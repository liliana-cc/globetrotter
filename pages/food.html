El Cardenal

Calle Palma 23 Cuauhtémoc, Centro, 06000 Ciudad de México, CDMX, Mexico

A beloved spot often recommended by tourists, El Cardenal is known for its delicious traditional Mexican breakfasts. Guests rave about the fresh bread and rich hot chocolate, while the enchiladas are praised for their balanced flavors and tender chicken. Expect a wait, but the food is well worth it.

Ram Ramen CDMX

Rosa de Castilla 114, Molino de Rosas, Álvaro Obregón, 01470 Ciudad de México, CDMX, Mexico

This ramen shop is a favorite for its flavorful broths and creative toppings. The suadero ramen and gyoza come highly recommended for their great taste. While some wish for leaner meat and more drink options, Ram Ramen delivers a satisfying and unique noodle experience in Mexico City.

El Vilsito

Avenida Universidad, Narvarte Poniente, 03020 Ciudad de México, CDMX, Mexico

Famous for its legendary tacos al pastor, El Vilsito is a must-visit taquería in Mexico City. The al pastor meat, expertly carved from the trompo, is juicy and flavorful. Don’t miss the Gringa—a flour tortilla with cheese, al pastor, and pineapple—a true highlight for taco lovers.
